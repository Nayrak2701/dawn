# Cold Traffic Conversion

Use this skill when planning homepage flow, PDP structure, trust bands, explainer sections, and any above-the-fold area intended for first-time paid traffic.

## First-visit mental checklist
Assume the visitor is asking:
1. What is this?
2. How does it work?
3. Why should I trust this?
4. Why should I want it?
5. Why should I buy now?

## Section strategy
A strong cold-traffic page usually includes:
- clear hero with product readability
- simple explanation of the Swop system
- trust/proof layer
- product/shop section
- aesthetic/lifestyle reinforcement
- FAQs or reassurance near buying moments

## Hero rules
The hero must communicate product category fast.
Do not sacrifice clarity for mood.
If the visual is ambiguous, add clearer product framing or supporting microcopy.

## Trust rules
Trust can come from:
- UGC
- review count or testimonials
- shipping / returns reassurance
- product explanation
- consistency of design and copy

Use multiple trust signals, not just one.

## Product explanation rules
SwopCase is not a generic case brand.
The swap mechanic must be shown, not only described.
Whenever possible, recommend visual explanation plus short supporting copy.

## Conversion writing rules
Prefer short, clear copy.
Avoid hype without proof.
Avoid celebrity-brand tone unless the page has enough trust to support it.

## Output format
For any page or section plan, provide:
- user question being answered
- section objective
- key elements
- asset needs
- trust contribution
- conversion risk if omitted
