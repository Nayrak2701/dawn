# SwopCase Theme

## Project purpose
This repository is a custom Shopify OS 2.0 storefront for SwopCase, a brand selling interchangeable phone case inserts called “Swops”.
The store is designed for cold TikTok traffic, so every important page must quickly communicate what the product is, how it works, why the store is trustworthy, and why the product feels aspirational.

## Non-negotiable brand rules
- Keep the global website background color at `#F1E9E9`.
- Do not recommend changing the overall site background.
- Design for a clean, feminine, warm, minimal premium aesthetic.
- Do not rely on celebrity-brand assumptions; trust must be built on-site.
- Prioritize clarity and trust before stylistic flourish.
- Mobile-first always.

## Visual rules
- Prefer hands, product details, flatlays, cropped lifestyle scenes, and realistic UGC over full AI-generated faces.
- When visuals are needed, define exact filenames, pixel dimensions, placement, and purpose.
- Use placeholder SVG assets first when final visuals are not ready.
- UGC sections should feel more real and trust-building than polished.

## Theme workflow
- Work section by section, not with a full-theme rewrite in one step.
- Before writing code, propose a short implementation plan.
- Use modular Shopify sections and settings that are understandable to non-developers.
- Prefer simple, maintainable Liquid, CSS, and minimal JavaScript.
- Do not remove trust-building sections for the sake of aesthetics.

## Conversion rules
- Every major homepage section should answer at least one key user question: what is it, how does it work, can I trust it, why do I want it.
- Product explanation must be visual as well as textual.
- Keep copy concise and clear.
- Optimize for first-time visitors from paid social.
